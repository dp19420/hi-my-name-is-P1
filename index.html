Childhood

I can remember using some form of the application paint on it, however, it required some sort of tiny floppy disk, 2 of them at the same time if I remember correctly. You could only color 1 pixel at a time, and there was no save function. But a new world soon opened up to me with the release of 2 groundbreaking games, Wolfenstein 3D and Duke Nukem 3D.
