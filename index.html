Hello and welcome to a page about me!

Childhood

My first computer was given to me by my father at around the age of 2, it was his old computer bought in the late 70s. I can remember using some form of the application paint on it, however, it required some sort of tiny floppy disk, 2 of them at the same time if I remember correctly. You could only color 1 pixel at a time, and there was no save function. But a new world soon opened up to me with the release of 2 groundbreaking games, Wolfenstein 3D and Duke Nukem 3D.


Growing up

Growing up i spent about equal time playing golf in the horse paddocks around my home as i did playin with computers. My father, having the fixation of having the latest technology of everything, i fixation i have iherited, brought home a new top of the line computer atleast every 2 years. placing the "old" one next to the new one for me to use. If i could tell you the amount of hours i've spent playing Red Alert or Football Manager it would probably lead you to question my tolerance for direct sunlight.
